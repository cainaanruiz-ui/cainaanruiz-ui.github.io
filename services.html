Immigration Psychological Evaluations

We provide professional, culturally sensitive immigration psychological evaluations to help support your case and strengthen your application.

Comprehensive Evaluations for:

  • Extreme Hardship Waivers (I-601 and I-601A)
  • Asylum Applications
  • U Visas for Victims of Crime
  • VAWA (Violence Against Women Act) Petitions
  • Cancellation of Removal Proceedings

What to Expect

  1. Initial Consultation: We’ll meet with you to understand your situation and what your case requires.
  2. Comprehensive Evaluation: We’ll explore history, current emotional state, and relevant experiences.
  3. Detailed Report: A professionally written report tailored to USCIS and immigration court standards.
  4. Attorney Collaboration: We can coordinate with your legal team to support your case goals.
