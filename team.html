Meet Our Team

Our clinicians provide culturally responsive care in a supportive, professional environment.

Luis E. Ruiz, LCSW

Clinical Director • Therapist

Luis is a bilingual (English/Spanish) Licensed Clinical Social Worker with years of experience providing counseling services focused on anxiety, depression, trauma, stress management, PTSD, and family and relationship concerns.

He believes therapy should help clients reach their goals and live fully through evidence-based psychotherapy and a collaborative therapeutic relationship.

Retired U.S. Army • Two combat tours in Iraq

Karen Sanchez, M.Ed.

Therapist

Karen brings over 10 years of counseling experience with high-trauma populations and specializes in supporting children and adolescents (ages 6–18) through emotional, behavioral, and developmental concerns.

She holds a Master’s degree in School Counseling from Liberty University and is currently pursuing her Doctorate in Counselor Education and Supervision. Karen is bilingual in English and Spanish and is passionate about accessible, inclusive care for diverse families.

Karen is honored to be part of the Happy 2 Help Counseling team and looks forward to supporting children and families on their journey toward growth and resilience.
