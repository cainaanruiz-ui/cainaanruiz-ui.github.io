What therapy can look like

Therapy is a collaborative space to explore thoughts, feelings, patterns, and goals—at a pace that feels safe.

Confidential

Sessions are private, with limited exceptions required by law (safety concerns, etc.).

Flexible

Weekly or biweekly sessions are common at first, then adjust as you progress.

Virtual options

Secure online sessions are available for convenience and accessibility.

FAQ-style basics

How long are sessions?

Many sessions are about 45–60 minutes, depending on the service and your needs.

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

If you want support, clarity, or tools for coping and growth, therapy can be a helpful place to start.

How do I schedule?

You can email or call to request a session, or use a booking link if you add one later.
