What Therapy Can Look Like

Therapy is a collaborative space to explore thoughts, feelings, patterns, and goals—at a pace that feels safe.

Confidential

Sessions are private, with limited exceptions required by law (such as safety concerns).

Flexible

Weekly or biweekly sessions are common at first, then adjust as you progress and your needs change.

Virtual Options

Secure online sessions are available to support accessibility and convenience.

FAQ Basics

How long are sessions?

Most sessions last between 45–60 minutes, depending on the service and your individual needs.

How do I know if therapy is right for me?

If you are seeking support, clarity, or tools for coping and growth, therapy can be a helpful place to begin.

How do I schedule?

You can contact us by phone or email to request a session, or use the client portal to get started.
